About Eunsu Choi

Eunsu Choi is an emerging composer, writer for musical theatre, and classically trained violinist based in Seoul, Republic of Korea.

Her evocative work received the Prime Minister Award in the "Her Story Contest" for its poignant portrayal of Korean 'comfort women.' Eunsu was also selected for the Musical Theatre Creative Talent Support Program, sponsored by HJ Culture and Korean Creative Contents Association. Her undergraduate education culminated in 'Departure: a staged song cycle,' the 30-minute song cycle that explores the emotional complexities associated with departure of many kinds, while reminiscing about the innocence of youth and contemplating the uncertainty of the future.

Eunsu aspires to be forever dedicated to the world of storytelling through music and theatre. She is a proud graduate of New York University Abu Dhabi.
