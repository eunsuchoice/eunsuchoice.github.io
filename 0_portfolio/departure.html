DEPARTURE: a staged song cycle

Departure is a staged song cycle that sheds light to different moments of departures in Jenna's life at her ages of 12, 24, and 36.

The 7 songs from this cycle depict physical departures, emotional detachments, someone else's departures that influence her and shape her. Jenna24 takes another departure herself to take a step beyond a stage of her life. In this process, Jenna24 engages and interacts with Jenna12 and Jenna36 on the stage, bringing the imagination out to real life.
