MOONLIGHT PHARMACY The Musical

Moonlight Pharmacy The Musical is a musical theatre work centered around a mysterious pharmacy that operates exclusively at night. The story follows a night owl female lead who stumbles upon the astonishing truth: the pharmacist undergoes a peculiar transformation during the day, returning to human form only by nightfall. At first, she playfully imagines him to be a vampire akin to Edward Cullen, but her perception takes an amusing twist when she learns that he actually transforms into a rabbit. Determined to help him break the enchantment, she embarks on a journey to restore his full humanity, all while unraveling the secrets of the enchanting "Moonlight Pharmacy.‚Äù

Drawing inspiration from the Chinese mythical archer Hou Yi and the Korean traditional legend of the 'moon rabbit,' the musical creatively reimagines two myths within a contemporary backdrop, aiming to evoke laughter and spark a sense of fantasy among its audience.
