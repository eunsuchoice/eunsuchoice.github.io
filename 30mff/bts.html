BTS: Our Most Important Prop for All, the 10 Dirham Bill

How Cole got his 10 dirham bill

At the time of the film shoot on September 2nd of 2021, all members discussed about an item that we all had. Then we realized Cole was the only one without a 10 dirham bill. So Eunsu, who lives in A6C, ran all the way to Cole, who lives in A5C, to drop off a bill.

How Eunsu's running turned out

Then later, Eunsu found out that one of her friends Harry was Cole's suitemate, and Harry mentions that he in fact had a 10 dirham bill at the time, so Eunsu's running all the way was a useless..

Did Eunsu get back her money?

Eunsu DID, thankfully. However, it took some time. Initially, Eunsu and Cole messaged each other and made sure they meet outside A6C. Eunsu went outside A6C and bumped into her friend, and while she was casually chatting, Cole passed by with his skateboard and disappeared. 10 minutes later, Cole walks into A6C, and it turns out that he had mistaken A6B for A6C.
