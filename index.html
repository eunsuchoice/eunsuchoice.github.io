30 Minute Film Festival is...

An ice-breaker as well as a creative curriculum of the Communications Lab course at NYU Abu Dhabi.
Students are assigned to different groups and are given 30 minutes to shoot and edit a film.
In Fall 2021, the theme was "Connections".
